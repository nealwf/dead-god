Intro

The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth is a 2014 roguelike dungeon crawler video game. Since 2014, it has gotten 3 expansions (also known as DLC for DownLoadable Content): Afterbirth, Afterbirth+, and Repentance. With all 3 DLC, the game has 637 achievements.

I play Rebirth through Steam, a game launcher that also tracks how many players have gotten a game's achievements. If less than 10% of players have an achievement, Steam considers it rare.

no historical or purchase data

For my final project, I wanted to find out more about Rebirth's rare achievements.

Note: Afterbirth+ got "booster packs" for over a year after its release that added more content to the game. Because there were achievements added by both base Afterbirth+ and Booster Pack 5 at different times, I've divided them out in my visualizations.

Disclaimer: if you have played Rebirth before, you may disagree with my analysis of the rarest achievements. I'd love to hear your thoughts.
